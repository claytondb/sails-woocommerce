## What You Need to Do (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create WordPress.org Account (if you don't have one)
1. Go to https://login.wordpress.org/register
2. Create account with username (this will be your plugin contributor name)
3. **Enable 2FA** at https://wordpress.org/support/users/profile/security/ (REQUIRED since 2024)

### Step 2: Submit the Plugin
1. Go to https://wordpress.org/plugins/developers/add/
2. Log in with your WordPress.org account
3. Upload the ZIP file: `C:\Users\clayt\OneDrive\Documents\sails-tax-woocommerce.zip`
4. Fill in:
   - **Plugin Name:** Sails Tax for WooCommerce
   - **Plugin Description:** Automatic sales tax calculation for WooCommerce stores powered by Sails.
5. Check the boxes agreeing to guidelines
6. Submit

### Step 3: Wait for Approval
- WordPress.org manually reviews all plugins
- Usually takes 1-5 business days
- They may email with questions or required changes
- Check your email (including spam folder)

### Step 4: After Approval
Once approved, you'll get SVN access. I can handle the SVN setup and future updates.
